Our Story

At CocoTech Craft, we're on a mission to transform discarded coconut shells into functional art. We believe in sustainable living, innovation, and empowering local artisans.

We are committed to upcycling waste, minimizing our environmental footprint, and promoting a circular economy. Every product reflects our dedication to a greener planet.

Our unique pieces are meticulously handcrafted by skilled local artisans, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern design principles. This ensures fair wages and supports community growth.
